Why join us

Autofox is creating a new type of auto service where mechanics can share in the success of their work. We believe mechanics should be valued for their hard work & expertise, and deserve a more fair option. If you’re tired of earning less while shops make more, then join us and keep your hard-earned money for you & your family.

Set your schedule

You have the freedom to work as much as you want. You provide us your availability and area of service, and we’ll book you appointments that meet your schedule.

Fair pay

Earn more. Our mechanics make an hourly rate of $40-50 an hour depending on their location, skills and work experience.

Simple jobs

We focus on simple repair and maintenance jobs like brakes, tire rotation, timing belts, alternators, cooling system, AC work, etc. No major jobs, period.

Hassle free

We take care of marketing, booking appointments and providing support, while you focus on the work at hand without any hassles.

Why does it work

Customers tell us their car issue

We organize the diagnostic or repair service required after collecting the make, model & year of the customer’s car.

We put together a quote

We prepare a clear and fair price quote for parts & labor with time slots of available mechanics for appointments.

You get the service appointmen

We’ll contact you with an appointment for a customer that needs the service you provide, at a time slot that meets your availability.

What Are The Requirements?

To be one of our mobile mechanics, you will need the following:

  • Basic tools of your own
  • A reliable vehicle and valid driver’s license
  • Experience as a licensed mechanic
  • A professional and friendly attitude
  • A clean background
